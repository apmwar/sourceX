Our Goal

If you’re a college student, just like us, chances are you’ve probably struggled in keeping your notes organised. They’re most commonly circulated through WhatsApp which doesn’t give you much choice to choose which folder to save them under.
Fear not! We here at sourceX strive to bring a change.
From barging you with constant updates about new notes to keeping a track of your reminders and schedule, we won’t ever let you feel disoriented.
The best part? Teachers can create an account too!
All this and more inside sourceX! Join us today!

What We Do
